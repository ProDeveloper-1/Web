About the system

-- Get to know about PRS --

The PRS (Problem Reporting System) is the electronic information system that helps students of the Institute of Finance Management (IFM) to quickly and easily report problems they daily encounter with in various enviroments surrounding them while they are at IFM.
Reports are just submitted online based to the enviroment the problem was found such as in classrooms, Washrooms, Computer labs, Hostels, Canteen, and Library.
Students fill the form found on the system to report the very problem specifically to area of conduct.
