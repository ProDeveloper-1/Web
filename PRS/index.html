Problem Reporting Sytem (PRS)

-- The easy way to problem solution --

Classrooms

The conducive and favourable environment for studying is the core foundation for the better performance of students in any area of study they pursue. Hence any problem concerning classrooms, report it.

Hostels

Favourable and conducive places of living contributes alot to the good pyschological health and better performance of students in any area of study they pursue.
Hence any problem concerning hostels, report it.

Library

The presence of learning materials at the institute (IFM) is a very crucial thing since it is the one of the sources of knowledge and understanding that students need to acquire. Therefore is better to report challenges faced in libraries.

Canteen

A nice and best food brings about the great sense of enjoyment and good body health of an individual. Each student would wish to have such kind of food. And for the sake of their health, it is good practice to report any problem found in canteens.

Computer Labs

Good technological infrastructure contributes alot to the great academic performance of students since computers give access to online courses and learning materials. As a matter of fact, it will be so great to report any challenge comes with computers.

Washrooms

The cleanliness of the washrooms is essential thing to be well observed, it saves life of many and cost for medical treatments is reduced since the number of sick people due to the dirtiness of washrooms reduced. Again any problem with washrooms has to be reported.
